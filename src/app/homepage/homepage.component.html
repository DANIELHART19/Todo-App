Todo App Instructions

To add a new todo, enter its title and body, then click the 'add todo' button

To edit an existing todo click its title, you will be directed to the editer, where you can change the title and todo body. Click 'Save' to save changes or 'Back' to stop editing.

Once the todo is completed, click its 'Done' button, which will remove and delete the todo.

Please click the link Todos to get started.
